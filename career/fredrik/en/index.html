Career

Fredrik Båberg

Ph.D. in Computer Science

Fredrik Båberg received the degree of Master of Science in Engineering, degree specialisation engineering physics, and Ph.D. degree in computer science from the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH), Stockholm, Sweden, in 2013 and 2023, respectively.

Fredriks Ph.D. studies was at the division of Computer Vision and Active Perception (CVAP), and later Robotics, Perception and Learning (RPL) at KTH where he was active in the EU FP7-project TRADR.
